Deformation quantisation workshop

2-4 June 2025, Centre for Quantum Mathematics, U230, SDU


In 1997, Kontsevich showed how to quantise Poisson manifolds. Our goal is to use recent work of Gaiotto-Kulp-Wu to investigate and generalise topological-holomorphic deformation quantisation through expository and working sessions

Organised by Alyosha Latyntsev and Guillaume Laplante-Anfossi
