    Notes

    1. Setting up definitions. Write down every variant of the KZ equation: KZ, boundary KZ, dynamical KZ, qKZ, and (q,t)-KZ equations. Develop the theory of differential, q-difference, and (q,t)-difference equations and their limiting behaviour.

    2. Finite quantum groups and associators. Construct the quasitriangular Hopf algebra Uq(g) from the KZ equations following [EK,Dr], and construct the braided monoidal category structure on Uq(g)-Mod.

    3. Multiple zeta values. Discuss the conjectural relationship between KZ associators and multiple zeta values.

    4. KZ from quantum field theory. Show how Atiyah-style TQFTs give rise to a local system on the category of line operators. Review [Co] and give an M-theory construction of (3/4/5)-dimensional Chern–Simons theory and hence the ( /q/q,t)-KZ equations from line operators. Make remarks about 3d N=2 theories, Hanany–Witten construction, and conjectures for arbitrary Calabi–Yau threefolds.

    5. Relation to Coulomb branches. Review the capping operator construction [Ok] of qKZ solutions from quasimaps. Show [Da] that KZ equations arise as Gauss–Manin connections on quantum cohomology of BFN Coulomb branches.

    6. KZ via Riemann–Hilbert and Kazhdan–Lusztig. Following [CF], show that the Kazhdan–Lusztig equivalence between vertex algebras and quantum groups provides two equivalent interpretations of KZ equations via the Riemann–Hilbert correspondence.

    Questions

    This list of questions will be updated, added to, as our understanding improves. It is meant as a tool for us to converge on what interesting open aspects there are in the subject at the moment. Many of the questions at the start will turn out to be trivial or noninteresting in the end.

    • Is there an analogue of the theory of Drinfeld KZ associators and the induced braided monoidal categories in the qKZ case? Is there the same relation between these and q-MZVs?
    • Does the KZ equation endow Rep U_q(g)—viewed as a sheaf of QCoh-module categories—with a connection, which via Riemann–Hilbert corresponds to its braided monoidal structure? What is the analogous story for q- and (q,t)-KZ?
    • Do q-MZVs appear from meromorphic associators in q-KZ?
    • [BCDN] have studied Kazhdan–Lusztig equivalences for other quantum groups/vertex algebras. What are the KZ equation analogues in this case? See [CF].
    • What is the critical point theory for the KZB equation, and how can Li–Zhou's regularised integral help with computations in the KZB case?
    • For the KZB equation, on the solution side, there are only very simple case studies on the elliptic parameter. Jie Zhou has done related work on the configuration space of elliptic curves and the mixed Hodge structure.
